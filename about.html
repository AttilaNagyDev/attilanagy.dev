About me

My journey into web development began with a curiosity about how websites and apps work behind the scenes. I was drawn in by the structure of clean HTML, the beauty of CSS transitions and animations, and the power of backend logic — especially when I started working with Python and Flask.

Since then, I’ve devoted much of my free time to learning the essential programming languages and tools, and I’ve become passionate about building full-stack projects from the ground up.

Along the way, I’ve gained hands-on experience with APIs, version control using Git and GitHub, and advanced image editing using Photoshop and Affinity Photo.

I’m currently focused on strengthening my backend skills, refining full-stack workflows, and learning about deployment and performance optimization. I love turning ideas into functional, user-friendly applications and constantly strive to improve both my code quality and design sense.

Next on my journey: learning JavaScript, exploring mobile development with Flutter, and getting familiar with WordPress for different business needs. This portfolio reflects the work I’ve done so far — and where I’m heading next.
